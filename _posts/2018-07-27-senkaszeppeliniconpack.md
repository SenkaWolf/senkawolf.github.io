## Installation
* Add my repo to cydia http://senkawolf.github.io/repo
* Click on 'Sources'.
* Click on 'Senka's Repo'.
* Select the 'Zeppelin Addon' Secton.
* Select the tweak 'Senka's Zeppelin Icon Pack'.
* Finally click 'install' at the top right.
     
That's all.
